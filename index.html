One of my favorite hobbies is listening and singing along to music.

Music
is defined as an artistic way of arranging sound to create a combination of form, harmony, melody, rhythm, or other expressive content. It is a part of every day life for many including myself. Music has a way to transform moods for the better and sometimes for the worse. It brings all of us together as it can manifest universal feelings that many times you cannot explain, you can just feel it. Music has always been my one constant in life and is healing to my soul.
Singing
is the act of creating musical sounds with your voice. Singing can be formal, informal, arranged, or even improvised. The sound of each individual's singing voice is entirely unique to them. Singing can be done as a profession, as a ritual, or even as a hobby. I once won a gold medal at a singing competition back in middle school. I used to be involved in choir throughout high school and now I sing for fun.
