Music
is defined as an artistic way of arranging sound to create a combination of form, harmony, melody, rhythm, or other expressive content. It is a part of every day life for many including myself. Music has a way to transform moods for the best and the worst. It can manifest feelings that sometimes one cannot explain and yet can be universal.
Music Genres
is a classification that identifies pieces of music into various categories as a shared tradition or set of conventions. A genre will have a distinguished musical style and form which can be used interchangeably. Some of the most popular genres we know today include the following: Country, Electronic, Funk, Hip Hop, Jazz, Latin, Pop, Punk, Reggae, Rock, Metal, Soul and R&B, Polka, Religious music, and Traditional and Folk music.
Singing
is the act of creating musical sounds with your voice. Singing can be formal, informal, arranged, or even improvised. The sound of each individual's singing voice is entirely unique to them. Singing can be done as a profession, as a ritual, or even as a hobby.
